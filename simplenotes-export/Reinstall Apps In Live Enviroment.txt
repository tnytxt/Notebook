Reinstall Apps In Live Enviroment

"Useful if you have deleted something that prevents your OS from booting."

**CTRL+ALT+F2** to reach the console mode to login with your user and password.

then...
`sudo apt-get --reinstall name-of package`

then enable it:
`systemctl enable name-of package`

reboot: 
`systemctl reboot`
