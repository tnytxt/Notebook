Enable a notification for Caps Lock

Open keyboard settings > application shortcuts > add new shortcut > enter below cmd.

`sh -c 'sleep 0.2; notify-send -t 5000  -i caps-lock "Caps Lock" $(xset -q | grep -oP "Caps Lock:\s*\K(off|on)")'`
