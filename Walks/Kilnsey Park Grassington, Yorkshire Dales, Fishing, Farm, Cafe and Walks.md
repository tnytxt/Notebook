## [WELCOME TO KILNSEY PARK](https://kilnseypark.co.uk/plan-visit/)

Kilnsey Park is a beautiful outdoor attraction near Grassington in the heart of the Yorkshire Dales. Relax and recharge amidst the green space and breathtaking views of Upper Wharfedale, Great Whernside and the towering Crag that inspired painter Turner. Share with your family the excitement of catching a fish for the first time in our springwater-fed lakes.
