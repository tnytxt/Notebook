The Hatch Brook Waterfall, Great Hill and Anglezarke Reservoir Loop - White Coppice is a 15.5 km looped trail that starts and ends in Chorley, Lancashire, England. Based on our data, the trail is graded as Medium. For information on how we grade trails, please read [measuring the difficulty of a hiking trail on hiiker](https://blog.hiiker.app/2020/07/08/measuring-the-difficulty-of-a-hiking-trail-on-hiiker/).
