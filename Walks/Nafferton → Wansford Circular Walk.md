#### Start: Nethergate, Nafferton
- Begin at the public footpath on Nethergate
- Narrow path through paddocks
- Hedgerows form a tunnel-like section
- Can be boggy in wet weather
#### Backcarr Lane → Hords Lane
- Path leads to Backcarr Lane
- Cross a level crossing
- Continue to Hords Lane and turn right
#### Towards Wansford
- Turn left onto Carr Lane
- Follow signs for Wansford
- Continue until the bridge
#### Nafferton Beck (Scenic section)
- Take public footpath before the bridge (right side)
- Walk alongside clear flowing water
- Trout often visible
####  Return to Nafferton
- Cross New Bridge Lane (near train station)
- Cross level crossing
- Walk fields to Priestgate
####  Finish: Crystal Clear Mere
- Located off Priestgate
- Spring-fed, very clear water
- Especially striking in sunshine
