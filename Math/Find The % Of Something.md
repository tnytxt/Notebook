Q. **what is 14% of 400**
A. 56

**How to find 14% of 400:**

1.  **Convert the percentage to a decimal:**
   14% is equivalent to 0.14 (14 divided by 100).
3.  **Multiply the decimal by the whole number:**
   0.14 multiplied by 400 equals 56.

3. Therefore, 14% of 400 is 56.


**A simpler way to find 14% of 400:**

1.  **Find 10%:** 10% of 400 is 40
   just move the decimal point one place to the left
3.  **Find 5%:**
   5% is half of 10%, so 5% of 400 is 20
4.  **Add the percentages:**
   14% is 10% + 4%, so add 40 (10%) and 20 (4%)

Therefore, 14% of 400 is 40 + 20 = 60.

This method can be helpful for mental calculations if you don't
have a calculator handy.
